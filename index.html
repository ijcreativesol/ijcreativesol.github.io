The withered cage

Of a flowering bush

In a bent posture

Fatigued of mortality

A mere portrayal

That remains

Of a spirited

Crimson past

A song that is now unsung

As a state of remembrance

And how delightful is it

To see a new leafy sprout

Young and sprightly

One that will flourish

To tell a new tale

For such has always been

The resolve of life
